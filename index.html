Mission Statement

To enhance the quality of life of orphanages and deprived communities in the aspect of education, health and basic needs (food, good drinking water, clothing and toiletries).


Vision Statement

Create a better life in the areas of education, health and basic needs (food, good drinking water, clothing and toiletries) for the less privileged.






What We Do (objectives)
  1. Register and renewal of NHIS cards.
  2. Create awareness on diseases and illnesses
  3. Provide educational aid like books, pens, pencils to orphanages and deprived communities
  4. Organize career fairs, conferences and guidance programs
  5. Provide food, water, toiletries and other orphanages and deprived communities
